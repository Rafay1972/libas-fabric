Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your fabric, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy.

Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • The fabric must be uncut, unwashed, and in its original packaging.
  • Sale items and custom-cut lengths (less than standard suit lengths) are non-refundable unless defective.

Process

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact us via WhatsApp at 0319-4327821 with your order details and reason for return. We will guide you through the process.

Defective Items

If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please notify us within 24 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free pickup and replacement immediately.
