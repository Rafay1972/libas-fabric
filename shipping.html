🚚 Shipping & Delivery

At لباسِ ہدایہ, we strive to deliver your favorite fabrics to your doorstep as quickly and safely as possible.

Delivery Areas

We deliver to all major cities across Pakistan including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, Quetta, and all other cities.

Delivery Charges

  • Orders above Rs. 3,000: Free delivery across Pakistan.
  • Orders below Rs. 3,000: A flat delivery charge of Rs. 200 applies.

Delivery Time

  • Major cities: 2-4 business days
  • Other areas: 5-7 business days

Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day. Tracking details will be shared via WhatsApp once dispatched.

Cash on Delivery

Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for all orders. Pay when you receive your fabric at your doorstep.
